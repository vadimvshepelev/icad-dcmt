Directions of Research

« Based on the world experience of the industrially advanced countries, i.e. USA and Europe, the long-term (25-30 years) Russian industrial policy should be aimed to creation and support of the backbone of high-tech industry, science and education in Russia — backbone companies — analogues of 900 US companies and, first of all, in such high-priority sectors as nuclear energy, aviation, shipbuilding. The main objectives of these companies for the next 10-15 years consists the implementation of the Russian business model «knowledge economy», the creation of world-competitive final products (nuclear power plants, aircraft, ships), capturing a significant share of the relevant niche of the world market, providing financial flows from the sale of serial products of stable employment and income state and system-formation companies.» Velikhov E.P., Betelin V.B., Kushnirenko A.G., Industrial policy, innovation, mass information technology, the domestic backbone of the company - Moscow: Energoizdat, 2007.

The main directions of scientific research in IAP RAS:

  • problems associated with the development of design automation systems (CAD) of large and ultra-large integrated circuits and electronic equipment;
  • problems associated with the study and development of CAD in mechanical engineering;
  • fundamental and applied problems of mathematical support of CAD and high-performance computers;
  • fundamental and applied problems of mathematical modeling of continuum mechanics and hydrophysics;
  • development of mathematical support of automated systems for special purposes and prediction of real situations.
